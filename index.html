MVHS Interact Club

Do you want to earn volunteer hours for your college applications?

Do you want to make a difference in other people's lives?

Do you want to get involved in your community?

Do you want to meet new people?

If so, Interact Club is the club for you!

Get updates through your phone by texting @mvhsic24 to 81010.

Interact Club is centered around giving back to the Highlands Ranch community while helping you achieve volunteer hours through numerous events spread throughout the year. In fact, colleges like to see "between 50 and 200 hours of volunteer work" on college applications (collegerealitycheck.com).

If you are interested, come to our back to school information meeting (date pending) where we announce our upcoming events and where you can sign up to become a member. You can participate in any amount of events you choose as long as you sign up on the "Events and Sign Ups" page for the events you will be attending. Hope to see you soon!

Event Descriptions

Club members visit Summit View Elementary during SOAR and advisement to read books to the first through third graders about anti-bullying techniques and kindness.

Club members visit the Life Care Center of Littleton where they do arts and crafts with the residents on Saturdays.

Club members visit the Project C.U.R.E. warehouse and sort donated medical supplies and package it to be sent to developing countries.
